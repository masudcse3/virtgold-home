    Diablo 4 HotA Barbarian Paragon Board

    The Paragon system becomes accessible when you reach level 50. Paragon Nodes offer a range of
    beneficial effects, from minor stats found in Normal and Magic Nodes to more potent and unique
    bonuses in Rare Nodes, Glyph Sockets, and Legendary Nodes.

    1. Starting Board
    2. Warbringer
    3. Flawless Technique
    4. Decimator
    5. Blood Rage
    6. Bone Breaker

    To simplify the process, let’s grasp the basic idea we’re aiming for. Our main focus is on Glyphs and
    the bonuses they provide. Once they reach level 15 or higher, they significantly enhance your build.
    Our strategy involves progressing through five boards and prioritizing the enhancement of our
    Glyphs. Along the way, we’ll also acquire a powerful Legendary Node called Warbinger, which
    greatly boosts the Fortify effect. The other Legendary Nodes may not be as valuable once you have
    a complete endgame build.

    Apart from that, we’ll pick up Rare Nodes when they contribute to our overall strategy, particularly if
    they provide stats that amplify separate multiplier categories such as Critical Strike Damage and
    Vulnerable Damage. As you progress through the boards, you can rearrange your Glyphs to
    optimize their placement. For example, in our final board, we have Wrath, which is an exceptionally
    powerful Glyph and should ideally be acquired earlier in the process.

    Diablo 4 HotA Barbarian Glyphs

    Paragon boards are characterized by the Glyphs they use. Glyphs are placed in sockets and offer
    massive benefits that increase with the required stats within their radius. Glyphs can be levelled up
    through successful Nightmare Dungeons completion. It’s crucial to focus on levelling the Wrath
    Glyph as it scales remarkably well. Here is a table showing the Glyphs we utilize in our build,
    ranked by levelling priority.

    Glyphs
    Description

    Expliot

    A truly powerful Glyph that automatically applies Vulnerable for 3 seconds. This is a separate multiplier that’s hard to acquire for us. It’s crucial to have.

    Wrath

    This Glyph, responsible for Critical Strike Damage, results in massive damage and cannot be overlooked. Although it’s the last socketed Glyph in the endgame board, it should be placed somewhere earlier to take advantage of its value before reaching level 100.

    Marshal

    This Glyph revives our Shouts, which is our main goal as a Barbarian. It’s placed in the Decimator board to intensify the Vulnerable nodes.

    Territorial

    This Glyph always deals damage to nearby targets. It’s placed in the Bone Breaker slot and can achieve 49 Dexterity, leading to +9.6% increased damage to close enemies, as well as 10% Damage Reduction against close enemies.

    Disembowel

    Due to all the nerfs to the shout cooldowns, this Glyph is needed to restore them. Each enemy killed will be bleeding from the Two-Handed Sword Expertise Technique Slot.

    Crusher

    This Glyph significantly enhances damage and can be greatly enhanced due to its Strength requirement.

    Once all your Glyphs hit level 15, continue levelling them to 21 in the same order. Keep in mind that
    other worthwhile glyphs, like Undaunted and Imbiber, may be considered for survival.

    Diablo 4 HotA Barbarian Season 1 – The
    Malignant

    In Diablo 4 Season 1, players embark on an exciting new questline to track down and defeat
    Malignant Monsters spread throughout the world of Sanctuary. These formidable foes hold a
    valuable prize: Caged Hearts. By acquiring these precious artifacts and placing them in Jewelry-
    infested sockets, players can unlock extraordinary powers that enhance and modify their
    character’s build. Check out our Diablo 4 Season of the Malignant guide!

    In Diablo 4 Season 1, there are a total of 32 Caged Hearts, each belonging to one of four distinct
    types, represented by different colors. These colors are:

  • Vicious (Orange): These Caged Hearts grant offensive powers, empowering your character’s
    attacks.
  • Brutal (Blue): Caged Hearts of this type provide defensive powers, bolstering your character’s
    resilience in battles.
  • Devious (Pink): Caged Hearts in this color offer utility powers, allowing your character to perform
    special actions and abilities.
  • Wrathful (Black): The Caged Hearts of this type are called “Super” hearts and possess
    extraordinary powers.

    • When socketing these Caged Hearts, there are specific rules to follow. The first three Caged Hearts
    must be placed into sockets that match their respective infested socket colors. However, the fourth
    Caged Heart can be inserted into any available infested socket.

    It’s important to note that once a Caged Heart is socketed, it becomes permanent and cannot be
    removed. However, if desired, they can be broken down into Invokers. These Invokers can be used
    to target farm specific Caged Hearts within the Malignant Tunnels.

    Keep in mind that the higher the level, the more powerful the Caged Hearts that will drop. So, as
    you progress and face more challenging enemies, you have a greater chance of obtaining stronger
    Caged Hearts to enhance your character’s abilities.
